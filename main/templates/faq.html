FAQ

Does Macchina communicate with the exchanges directly?

Yes, Macchina is built on top of a wrapper for all the APIs of the exchanges it supports, which are more than 100. At the moment this wrapper is not public but we may consider open-sourcing it in the future.

What happens if an arbitrage opportunity is no longer profitable?

Macchina monitors arbitrage opportunities periodically and when it detects that an arbitrage opportunity is no longer profitable it will delete it from the pool of the current arbitrage opportunities.

What happens if an exchange disappears?

If Macchina cannot communicate with an exchange, arbitrage opportunities will become old and they will be automatically removed from the pool of the current arbitrage opportunities.
